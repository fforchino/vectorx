Vector Onboarding

To use your bots with VectorX and Wirepod, you need to onboard them, i.e. telling them to connect them to your RPi rather than using any server on the internet.

This step needs to be done only once per robot. To onboard, open this link with Google Chrome:

https://keriganc.com/vector-epod-setup/html/main.html


and follow the instructions. Repeat for any other bot you may want to add, then click the button below to finish.
